Core Ideas

Eight operating principles that repeat across the letters

Customer Obsession

Start from customer pain points and work backward to product choices.

Day 1 Mindset

Stay curious, fast, and externally focused to avoid organizational drift.

Long-Term Thinking

Prefer durable compounding over near-term optimization.

Invention Culture

Require experimentation, and treat failed experiments as tuition.

High Standards

Raise quality bars through clear expectations and constant calibration.

Decision Velocity

Use reversible decisions quickly; reserve deep process for irreversible ones.

Ownership

Think like builders with responsibility beyond your immediate team.

Operational Excellence

Pair ambition with disciplined execution and measurable outcomes.
